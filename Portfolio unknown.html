The Unknown

digital drawing

Digital drawing educates people on ADHD. Many people do not know about learning disability therefore they act harshly. I have ADHD and have been labelled and shunned for it. ADHD does not make me different and I want to make people aware of this with my drawing.

(Bianca Gauthier, January 2019, 22x31inch, Digital Drawing)
