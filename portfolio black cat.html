The Black Cat

Acrylic Panting

Using bold contrasting colours to touch on prejudice, like black cats connected to bad luck and cats generally labelled as unfriendly. A duality between opinion and reality is expressed by a flow of colour representing the positive aspect that are overlooked.

(Bianca Gauthier, January 2023, 16x20 inch, Acrylic Painting)
