A Memorable Showtime

Acrylic Painting

The subject matter of this painting was fond memories. Before I was a the artist that I am today, I started as a Dancer. Dancing helped me to express myself. It was something that was very dear to me. Once in the past during one of my shows, I ended up dancing right next to my grandmother. The joy that I saw when she looked at me dancing is such an important memory for me. I remember her telling me how she got so excited that she wanted to touch me but was scared that she would interupt my dancing. My grandmother supports me all the way with my work just like my family and this fond memorie now lives on canvas.

(Bianca Gauthier, Feburary 2022, Digital Drawing)
