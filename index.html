Who I am

Traditional and Digital Artist

I have been creative seen I was young when I discovered dancing. It took me time before discovered the flourishing of my art when I decided to study in the Art in College. I am an artist who finds excitement in trying new ways of making art. Many of my work touches on personal aspects on my Life I am inspired by the people surrounding me and my environment. Memories and identity are big subjects in my work.

I find my work as a way to express myself when I have a hard time saying them. Some of my work touches on my disabilities, from ADHD, Dyslexia, chronic migraine, nerve damage and so on. My work is an outlet where I can express some of the pain or just accept living with them. Not all my works are about them but I feel that my ideas are impacted by what affects me in my everyday life.
