Invisible Purple Acceptance

Acrylic painting

This painting is a self-portraite. This painting depicts some of my non-visual disabilities. I have chronic Migraines and Nerve Damage from having shingles at the age of 19. Specifically these two disabilities effects my day-to-day life, as they effect my productivity and more. Therefore, with art I have to adapte. With art and my family, I have been able to adapt to these new changes, however these are still daily pains that i live with. I have come with an acceptance of these new changes and this painting dosent show how painfull it is. This demonstrate my tiredness and acceptance. The purple for me help depict the look of my migrain and nerves, I also wnated to depicted the specific places effected with these small nerve like lines.

(Bianca Gauthier, September 2023, 30x40 inch, Acrylic Painting)
