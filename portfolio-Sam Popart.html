Was It You?

Digital drawing

Digital drawing on Photoshop. Based on a character from one of my past photographic works. It utilizes the aspect of the inspector in Film Noir. It takes the concept of the harsh lighting in film noir and draws only the part of the body that the light hits. This work tries to make the viewer try and see if it is a good or bad character, bringing forth the different faces of investigators in Film Noir.

(Bianca Gauthier, April 2021, 18x24 inch, Digital Drawing)
