Blooming Flower

sculpture

A clay sculpture representing the motion of a delicate flower blooming to its full beauty. When doing this sculpture I wanted to bring the idea of a fleeting moment in time. Many things are beautiful or amazing but that is overlooked because of how small they are.

(Bianca Gauthier, November 2020,, Clay sculpture)
